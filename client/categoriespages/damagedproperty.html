Damaged Property Report

We are committed to addressing damaged property issues promptly and effectively in our community. Ensuring the safety and aesthetics of our neighborhood is a shared responsibility, and we appreciate your cooperation.

Damaged Property Assessment:

  • Our dedicated team assesses reported damaged property concerns in a timely manner.
  • We prioritize urgent situations and take appropriate actions to prevent any potential hazards.

Types of Damaged Property:

  • We handle a wide range of damaged property issues, including but not limited to:
    • Vandalized public structures
    • Graffiti on public buildings
    • Broken streetlights
    • Damaged park benches
    • Defaced signs and notices

Reporting Damaged Property:

  • Please provide detailed information about the location and nature of the damage.

Help Us Maintain a Safe Environment:

  • Your vigilance and reports play a vital role in maintaining the quality of our neighborhood.
  • By reporting damaged property, you contribute to the overall well-being of our community.

To report a damaged property issue or for more information, please use our online reporting tool. Together, we can ensure our community remains a safe and pleasant place to live and work.
