Illegal Parking in Florin County Council

We take illegal parking issues seriously and are dedicated to maintaining safety and order within Florin County Council. Your cooperation in ensuring proper parking practices is greatly appreciated.

Illegal Parking Concerns:

  • We actively address reported cases of illegal parking to maintain traffic flow and pedestrian safety.
  • Your reports help us identify problem areas and take appropriate actions.

Types of Illegal Parking:

  • We address various forms of illegal parking, including but not limited to:
    • Blocking fire hydrants and emergency access points
    • Parking in handicap-designated spots without proper permits
    • Overstaying time limits in restricted zones
    • Double parking that obstructs traffic

Reporting Illegal Parking:

  • You can report instances of illegal parking through our online reporting system.
  • Please provide detailed information about the location, vehicle description, and the nature of the violation.

Our Commitment to You:

  • Upon receiving your report, we work diligently to investigate and address illegal parking concerns promptly.
  • Our goal is to maintain the safety and accessibility of public spaces.

Help Us Ensure a Safe Community:

  • Your vigilance and reports are essential in maintaining order and safety in Florin County Council.
  • By reporting illegal parking, you contribute to a better and safer environment for all residents.

To report an illegal parking issue or for more information, please use our online reporting tool. Together, we can ensure the continued safety and convenience of our community.
