Potholes Report

We take the issue of potholes seriously and are committed to maintaining safe road conditions within our community. Your assistance in reporting potholes is greatly appreciated.

Pothole Assessment:

  • Our dedicated team assesses reported potholes promptly to ensure road safety and prevent damage to vehicles.
  • We prioritize the repair of potholes based on severity and location.

Types of Potholes:

  • We address various types of potholes, including but not limited to:
    • Small cracks that develop into potholes
    • Deep and wide potholes that pose hazards to drivers
    • Potholes in high-traffic areas that require urgent attention

Reporting Potholes:

  • You can report potholes in the road through our online reporting system.
  • Please provide specific details about the pothole's location, size, and any safety concerns it poses.

Our Commitment to You:

  • Upon receiving your report, we prioritize the repair of potholes to ensure road safety and convenience for all residents.
  • We work diligently to maintain the quality of our roads and prevent further damage.

Help Us Maintain Safe Roads:

  • Your reports are essential in identifying and addressing potholes promptly.
  • By reporting potholes, you contribute to safer road conditions for everyone in our community.

To report a pothole issue or for more information, please use our online reporting tool. Together, we can ensure safe and smooth roads for our community.
