Graffiti

Graffiti are drawings, symbols, messages or 'tags' that are painted,

written, stencilled or carved on walls and other surfaces.  Graffiti can make an area look untidy and feel unsafe. It can also be

offensive.

Before you report graffiti to us, it’s helpful if you can take photos of

the graffiti and make a note of where it is and the time when you first saw it. We can use this as evidence if we arrest and charge someone for the offence.
