Streets not Swept

We are committed to maintaining clean and well-kept streets in our community. Here's how we handle street sweeping:

Residential Streets

We sweep every residential street at least once a week to ensure cleanliness and safety. Our efforts include:

  • Continuous sweeping of busy shopping areas, including Riverside Avenue, Willowbrook Lane, Maplewood Street, Sunflower Boulevard, Brookside Drive, Cedarwood Crescent, Meadowview Lane, and Oakridge Avenue between 6 am to 10 pm, seven days a week.
  • Use of mechanical sweeping machines on main roads, which also assist in sweeping residential streets.
  • Weed removal by our staff as part of the sweeping process, along with a program of weed spraying carried out three times a year.
  • Cleaning our busy parks of litter during the summer six days a week.

Main Highways

Main highways are cleaned daily, both in the mornings and again in the afternoon, to maintain safe and accessible road conditions.

We are dedicated to providing a clean and inviting environment for our residents and visitors. If you have concerns or spot areas that require attention, please feel free to contact us.

Reporting Unswept Streets:

  • If you've observed streets that have not been swept according to the schedule, please report them through our online reporting system.
  • Provide specific details about the location and the date when you noticed the issue.
  • Your reports help us identify areas that require immediate attention.

Our Commitment to Clean Streets:

  • Upon receiving your report, we will investigate and take necessary action to ensure streets are swept according to the schedule.
  • We value the cleanliness and appearance of our community.

Help Us Maintain Clean Streets:

  • Your reports are crucial in helping us uphold our commitment to clean and well-maintained streets.
  • By reporting unswept streets, you contribute to the overall cleanliness and aesthetics of our community.

To report unswept streets or for more information, please use our online reporting tool. Together, we can ensure our streets remain clean and inviting for all residents and visitors.
